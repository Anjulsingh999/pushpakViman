Drone

3-Days Workshop

Explore drones comprehensively in our 3-day workshop: grasp the basics, including components and technology; engage in hands-on assembly, safety, and flight training; delve into real-world applications across industries; and conclude with project work and insights into future drone trends.

1-Week Workshop

Step into a dynamic 1-week Fusion 360 Workshop, where we uncover the potential of comprehensive design and CAD tools. Through basic sketching, hands-on exercises, simulation, and 3D printing essentials, our interactive sessions empower you to craft, simulate, and breathe life into your designs. Experience a week of creativity, practical learning, and a journey into the future of design trends.

Design & 3D Printing

1-Week Workshop

Step into an exciting 1-week 'Fusion 360 Workshop,' where we unfold the potential of comprehensive design tools. From initial sketches to hands-on exercises, simulation, and essential 3D printing insights, participants will dive into the realms of creation, simulation, and design realization. Join us for an immersive experience, exploring innovation, practical learning, and the future of design trends.

2-Weeks Workshop

Dive into a comprehensive 2-week design and 3D Printing Essentials Workshop. Explore the history, applications, and technology of 3D printing, learn beginner-friendly design tools, and gain hands-on experience in slicing software. In the design segment, journey from Fusion 360 basics to hands-on exercises, simulations, and CAM fundamentals, culminating in a showcase of participants' projects. Join us for an immersive exploration of design and 3D printing innovations.

1-Month Workshop

Dive into a month-long design and 3D Printing Essentials Workshop, where you'll journey from the basics to advanced skills. Explore Fusion 360's comprehensive design features, hands-on exercises, simulations, and delve into 3D printing essentials. From sketching to CNC machining, this immersive experience culminates in a project showcase, providing participants with a comprehensive skill set and the opportunity to bring their creations to life.
