3-Days Workshop on Drone
Day 1: Introduction to Drone and Basic Concepts
Session 1:Introduction to Drone
  • Overview of the workshop agenda
  • Types of drones and their history
  • Applications of drones in various industries
Session 2: Drone Components and Technology
  • Overview of drone hardware (frames, motors, propellers, etc.)
  • Introduction to drone software and control systems
  • Understanding sensors and communication systems
Day 2: Hands-on Drone Assembly and Flight Basics
Session 1: Basic Drone Assembly and Safety
  • Participants get the opportunity to assemble a basic drone.
  • Importance of responsible drone operation
  • Risk management and pre-flight checklists
  • Hands-on exercises with simulator software for drone control practice
Session 2: Outdoor Flight Training
  • Practical flight demonstrations
  • Participants get hands-on experience flying drones under supervision.
  • Autonomous flight modes and features
Day 3: Real-world Applications and Future Trends
Session 1: Drone Applications in Various Industries
  • Case studies on how drones are used in agriculture, surveying, filmmaking, etc.
  • Guest speakers from relevant industries sharing their experiences.
  • Drone Data Collection and Analysis
Session 2: Project Work and Closing
  • Discussion on the future role of drones in society
  • About Drone Training Program & Eligibility
  • Participants work on a small drone-related project.
  • Presentations and discussions on the projects.
  • Closing ceremony, certificates, and feedback collection.
